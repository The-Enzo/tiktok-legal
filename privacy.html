Privacy Policy

App: WouldYouCheckThisOut  |  Operator: thebotspot.net

Last updated: June 16, 2026

WouldYouCheckThisOut ("the App", "we", "us") is a content-creation tool that generates short animated avatar videos and publishes them to the TikTok account of the person who operates it. This policy explains what data we access and how we handle it.

1. Information we access

  • TikTok account authorization. When you connect your TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit, we receive an OAuth access token and refresh token that let the App act on your behalf for the scopes you approve.
  • Basic profile (user.info.basic). Your TikTok open ID, display name, and avatar, used only to confirm which account is connected.
  • Video upload/publish (video.upload / video.publish). Used solely to upload videos that you generate with the App to your own TikTok account.

2. How we use it

We use this access only to (a) confirm your connected account and (b) upload videos you create to your TikTok account or drafts. We do not use it for any other purpose.

3. Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the operator's own machine in a private configuration file. They are not transmitted to any third party. Generated videos are stored locally and are only sent to TikTok when you choose to post.

4. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is exchanged only between the App (on your machine) and TikTok's official APIs.

5. AI-generated content

Videos produced by the App are AI-generated and are disclosed to TikTok as such (is_aigc) when posted.

6. Retention & revocation

You can revoke the App's access at any time from your TikTok account settings (Security & permissions → Manage app permissions). Deleting the local configuration removes the stored tokens.
